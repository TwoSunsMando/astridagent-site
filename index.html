SMS Messaging Policy

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

This SMS Messaging Policy describes how AstridAgent AI LLC and its products (including SmartBook) use SMS text messaging to communicate with end users on behalf of the businesses that use our platform.

By providing your phone number and sending a text message to a business powered by AstridAgent AI / SmartBook, you consent to receive SMS messages related to:

  • Appointment and service scheduling confirmations
  • Service quotes and estimates
  • Appointment reminders

Message frequency varies based on your interactions. Automated reminders are typically limited to 1-2 messages per scheduled appointment.

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive one confirmation and no further messages. To resume, text START.

Standard message and data rates may apply. AstridAgent AI does not charge end users for receiving SMS messages.

6. Carriers

Messages are sent via major US carriers including AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, and Sprint. Delivery is subject to carrier availability.
